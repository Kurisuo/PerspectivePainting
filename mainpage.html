Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to paint my house?

The cost varies depending on the size of your home, the type of paint used, and the complexity of the project. We provide free, detailed estimates after an on-site evaluation. Generally, interior painting ranges from $2-6 per square foot, while exterior painting ranges from $3-7 per square foot.

How long does a typical painting project take?

Most residential interior projects take 2-5 days, while exterior projects typically take 3-7 days. Commercial projects vary based on size and scope. We always provide a detailed timeline during the estimate process and work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine.

What type of paint do you use?

We use premium paints from trusted brands like Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore. These high-quality paints provide excellent coverage, durability, and are resistant to fading, cracking, and peeling. We can also accommodate specific brand preferences based on your needs.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes! We stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. Interior painting comes with a 5-year warranty, exterior painting includes a 5-10 year warranty depending on the number of coats applied, and cabinet painting includes a 2-year warranty. Our warranties cover both labor and materials.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Perspective Painting is fully licensed (CSLB LIC #1044037) and carries comprehensive insurance including $2 million in liability coverage. All our employees are covered by Workers' Compensation insurance, giving you complete peace of mind during your project.
